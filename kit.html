Welcome to KIT:Kirirom Institute of Technology


What is KIT?

Kirirom Institute of Technology, aka KIT, is a private university located in Cambodia's hidden paradise, Kirirom National Park. The university was established in 2014 offering one field of study, the Department of Software Engineering where nature and IT are fused, and later followed by the Department of Tourism Management and Architecture. The Master's of Software Engineering program will be open in May 2023.


Bacheolor's program

Software Engineering

The Department of Software Engineering emphasizes on raising the standards of education by introducing cutting-edge technology based on information engineering and implementing it in real life.

Tourism Management

The Tourism Management Program promotes an understanding of successful management and leadership practice in tourism and hospitality contexts. Students gain actual industry experience during the internship or work placement semester. In addition, this course promotes continuous engagement with industry experts.

Architecture

The Architecture Program is a diverse and competitive program within interdisciplinary skills development such as Art, Mathematics, Science, Technology, and Innovation combined with creativity and an understanding of social needs, cultural trends, and climatic flexion.
